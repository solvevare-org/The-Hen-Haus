About Us

Hen Haus connects clients and providers, making it easy, transparent, and affordable to get the help you need. Whether you’re a homeowner looking for project support or a contractor expanding your team, you can post jobs, compare bids, and find the right pro for your budget. Service providers—including tradespeople and independent professionals—can clearly state their rates, discover new opportunities, and avoid the high platform fees of other apps. We’re here to keep things simple, straightforward, and community-driven—true to the spirit of our name—while helping build strong local connections that work for everyone.
